Maintelex CMMS

Customer Relations Management (CRM)

Developed a customer CRM using Google Sheets and Google AppScript to help sales reps log interactions. The CRM allowed users to add products of interest, provide quotes, access product specs, and view competitor information. Data was archived for later sales analytics. Purchase Request Tool

Developed a tool to streamline the purchasing process. It checks if the requested item is within the requester’s approved limit and the department's budget. If it is, the request goes to procurement; if not, it seeks necessary approvals before proceeding. After purchase, the cost is deducted from the department's budget, and the requester is notified of the tracking status and arrival.

Developed a Quality Assurance scorecard using Google Sheets and AppScript for auditing customer calls. The scorecard linked to the call database, attaching MP3 recordings for reference. After grading, scores and comments were sent to the advisor and manager, and a record was saved in the master database.

Label Updater
